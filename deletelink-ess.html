Delete Your Account and Data - 360 Solve ESS

If you would like to delete your 360 Solve ESS account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  • Open the 360Solve ESS app.
  • Go to the account settings section in the app.
  • Select "Delete Account".
  • Confirm your choice to delete your account.

Data Deletion Information

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, contact details)
  • Attendance records and leave requests
  • Profile settings and preferences

Any data required for compliance with legal obligations will be retained for 30 days before being permanently deleted.
